   These predictions fall within the sensitivity of existing experimental platforms.

   The scalar–curvature coupling \( \lambda \Phi R \) yields an effective equation of state \( w_\Phi \approx -1 \), aligning with established scalar‑tensor and dark‑energy frameworks while introducing aperiodic structure as a new organizing principle.


5. A minimal Lagrangian with no free parameters.  
   All characteristic scales — including the phason mass, modulation constant, and natural field amplitude — are derived from fundamental constants. The theory introduces no arbitrary potentials or tunable parameters.


The manuscript is positioned at the intersection of condensed‑matter theory, mathematical physics, and cosmology. It synthesizes established results from quasicrystal physics, scalar‑field cosmology, and precision measurement science into a coherent framework with clear experimental pathways for validation or falsification.


Given the interdisciplinary nature of the work and its grounding in recent advances in aperiodic order and scalar‑field phenomenology, I believe it will be of interest to readers working in unified field theory, topological matter, and precision tests of fundamental physics.
